About Us

Muna Therapy Center is a professional rehabilitation and wellness facility located in Bweyogerere, opposite Mamerito Hotel, just 300M off the Jinja–Kampala Highway. We provide specialized therapy services to both children and adults, focusing on improving daily living skills, communication, mobility, emotional well-being, and overall quality of life.

Our Vision

Leading the Future of Rehabilitation in Uganda.
To be the most trusted and foremost rehabilitation center in Uganda, empowering individuals of all ages to reclaim their fullest potential. We aim to dismantle stigma, foster inclusion, and help every person build a life of meaning, purpose, and independence.

Our Mission

Excellence in Compassionate Care.
To deliver high-quality, evidence-based, and culturally sensitive therapy services in a safe and compassionate environment where every client feels valued and supported.

Our Core Values

The Pillars of Our Leadership
These principles guide every decision and reflect our commitment to exceptional care:

  • Compassion and Empathy (Obuntu): Caring for every person with understanding and respect for their unique journey.
  • Excellence and Integrity: Maintaining the highest standards of professional and ethical practice.
  • Innovation: Adopting modern, evidence-based approaches to achieve better outcomes.
  • Inclusion and Dignity: Treating everyone with equality, respect, and cultural sensitivity.
  • Empowerment: Equipping clients with confidence and independence to thrive beyond therapy.

Holistic Rehabilitation for Children and Adults

We are dedicated to offering supportive, person-centered therapy services that empower individuals to reach their fullest potential. Our care goes beyond treatment, we partner with families to build confidence, independence, and long-term well-being.

Our multidisciplinary team provides Occupational Therapy, Speech and Language Therapy, Behavioral Therapy, Physiotherapy, Nutrition services, and Psychotherapy. Each therapist is professionally trained, licensed, and registered with the Uganda Allied Health Professionals Council, ensuring ethical, evidence-based and high-quality care.
