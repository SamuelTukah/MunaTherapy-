Services

We offer a comprehensive range of rehabilitation and therapeutic services designed to support physical, emotional, cognitive, and behavioral well-being. Our approach focuses on personalized treatment plans that empower individuals of all ages to live more independent and fulfilling lives.

  1. Home
  2. Services

Occupational Therapy

We help individuals develop, recover, and maintain essential daily living and work skills. Therapy sessions are tailored to address physical, mental, cognitive, and developmental challenges.

Daily living and self-care training Physical and cognitive rehabilitation

Speech & Language Therapy

We evaluate, diagnose, and treat speech, language, communication, and swallowing disorders. Our goal is to enhance communication and cognitive-communication skills while improving swallowing function.

Speech and language exercises & AAC support Therapy for articulation, language, and neurological conditions

Physiotherapy

We work to restore and improve mobility, balance, and overall physical function. Our physiotherapists use targeted exercises, manual therapy, and education to support full recovery.

Musculoskeletal & neurological rehabilitation Pain management and functional mobility

Behavioral Therapy

Evidence-based behavior assessment and intervention aimed at understanding behaviors, improving emotional regulation, and supporting positive change.

Behavior Assessment & Intervention Plans Support for Emotional & Social Skills

Nutrition Services

Personalized dietary guidance and meal planning to support healthy living, disease management, and optimal well-being.

Personalized Diet Plans Health & Wellness Nutrition Guidance

Psychotherapy & Counseling

Supportive mental health care focused on emotional well-being, stress management, coping strategies, and personal growth.

Individual & Family Counseling Mental Health & Emotional Support
