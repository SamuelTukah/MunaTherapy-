Muna Therapy Center is a registered rehabilitation and therapy facility licensed by the Uganda Allied Health Professionals Council. Our multidisciplinary team includes Occupational Therapists, Speech & Language Therapists, Behavioral Therapists, Physiotherapists, Nutritionists, Psychotherapists, and Counselors.

We use a holistic, client-centered approach to address physical, cognitive, behavioral, and emotional needs, ensuring every client receives targeted therapy aligned with their goals.

Frequenty Asked Questions

01

What services does Muna Therapy Center offer?

We provide a range of therapy services including counseling for stress, anxiety, trauma, family issues, child therapy, and wellness coaching. Each session is tailored to meet the individual needs of our clients.

02

How do I book an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment through our website, by phone, or by visiting our center. Once booked, one of our support staff will confirm your session time and any necessary details.

03

Are therapy sessions confidential?

Yes. All sessions are strictly confidential. We adhere to professional ethical standards to protect your privacy and ensure a safe and trusting environment.

04

Do I need a referral to start therapy?

No referral is required. You can directly contact us and begin your journey. Our therapists will guide you through the first steps and help you determine the most suitable therapy approach.
